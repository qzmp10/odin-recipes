Cheesecake recipe


Mom's cheesecake

Mom's cheesecake recipe is a creamy cheesecake with the following ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup margarine
  • 3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups sour cream

Here are the steps to follow to make the cheesecake:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Fahrenheit
  2. In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, 3 tablespoons sugar, cinnamon and melted margarine. Mix all ingredients well and press into bottom and sides of a 9 inch springform pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Blend in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla and salt. Fold in the sour cream.
  4. Pour batter into springform pan. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour. Turn off oven and leave cake in for at least 30 minutes.
