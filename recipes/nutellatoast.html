Nutella toast recipe

Nutella toast

Not only is nutella toast delicious, it is also easy to make! Let's jump in!

Required ingredients

  • White bread
  • Nutella

Here are the steps to follow to recreate the recipe!

  1. Put your bread in the toaster
  2. Wait 3 minutes
  3. Take your bread out of the toaster.Watch out it's hot!
  4. Spread nutella on it
  5. Enjoy!
