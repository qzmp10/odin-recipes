Mac'N'Cheese Recipe

Ranch Mac'n'Cheese recipe

Mac'n'cheese with a side of ranch! Let's jump in!

Ingredients required

  • 3 cups gemelli pasta, uncooked
  • 1 (5 ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 1 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing
  • 2 large eggs eggs, slightly beaten
  • 3 cups grated Asiago cheese
  • ½ cup unseasoned bread crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
